Payment Options


Your Time or Your Money


Here are some payment options available to you


Payment Plan

Any individual is eligible for a payment plan. Payment plans should be set up within 10 days after the date of the court disposition. You will not receive an additional fine for setting up a payment plan.

Community Service

An alternative sentencing option, for those interested or unable to pay, that allows citizens the opportunity to contribute approved community service hours to an government volunteer program in exchange for some or all of a fine.
